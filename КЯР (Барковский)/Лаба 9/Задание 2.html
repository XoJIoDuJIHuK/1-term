Manufactured by the Lastimosa Armory and commissioned by the IMC, the R-301 is the successor to the R-101 and R-201 rifles from the Frontier War. In the years after the Battle of Typhon, the IMC felt a need to improve on their very successful and reliable R-201 platform. High volumes of data collected from the R-201 and the R-201-A2's internal CPU's indicated that most mechanical failures were due to the rifle frame's exposure to extreme elements which caused misfires and the occasional feed mechanism malfunction. The internal components have been revised with a lighter and stronger composite as the external framework has been strengthened with a composite metallic weave to better compensate for overheating and structural integrity. The piston system was removed overall and the bolt cycling mechanism is completely controlled by the R-301's integrated AI. What you have with this new and improved platform is a reliable and accurate rifle with minimal maintenance and a larger caliber.

The Rampage is a light machine gun introduced in Season 10 that utilizes Heavy Rounds. The Rampage can consume a Thermite Grenade to temporarily be Revved Up Revved Up. Consuming the grenade and revving up takes 4 seconds. The charge lasts for 90 seconds, but each shot will reduce the timer by 2 seconds. Revved Up grants a 30% increase in fire rate and the ability to damage doors. The door damage is half of the damage done by a melee attack. Four shots will destroy a door from full health.

The Triple Take is a triple-barreled marksman rifle that utilizes Supply Drop Energy Ammo. It shoots 3 projectiles in one shot, one at the center and two to its sides, whose spread can be reduced while aiming down sights when using the choke fire mode. Being a Supply Drop Weapon, it can only be found in Care Packages, and cannot be equipped with foreign ammo. It comes pre-equipped with 63 backup ammo, an Extended Energy Mag, and a 2x-4x Variable AOG scope.

The Mozambique Shotgun is a fully automatic triple-barrel shotgun pistol that utilizes Shotgun Shells. It shoots three pellets in the shape of a triangle. It has an April Fools variant that can only be found on April 1st, with a larger magazine, faster fire rate, and higher damage per pellet.
